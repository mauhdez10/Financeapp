# GTM — Advisor / Agent Recruiting Pitch

> Audience: bilingual insurance agents and money coaches we want on the platform.
> US-based today, not US-limited in framing — the model travels anywhere there are
> bilingual advisors serving underserved families.
> Compliance framing throughout (D-17): the platform delivers **financial education and
> coaching tools** — not investment advice, not money management. Each advisor owns
> their own licensing and compliance; Golden Anchor provides the operating system.

---

## 1. The pitch in one paragraph

You're a bilingual agent or money coach. You're great with people — and you're drowning
in everything else: spreadsheets, intake forms, follow-ups, payment links, reports built
by hand at midnight. The big tech platforms weren't built for you or your clients —
they're English-only and priced for the mass-affluent. **Golden Anchor is Advisor-in-a-
Box: the operating system for bilingual financial coaches.** You bring the relationships
and the license; we bring the entire digital practice — your own branded client portal,
CRM, reports, intake, payments, and referral network — in English and Spanish, from
day one.

## 2. What "Advisor-in-a-Box" means — your own portal

Everything below is the same production system Golden Anchor's own practice runs on
today (live at finance.goldenanchor.life), being productized per advisor:

- **Bilingual CRM** — every client, profile, goals, notes, and history in one place;
  every screen in EN/ES.
- **Client-facing app** — your clients get their own login: dashboards, charts,
  calculators with their real numbers, learning resources. Free tier for everyone,
  Premium self-serve upsell built in.
- **Professional reports** — Complete Reports and month-to-month comparisons, generated
  from client data, PDF-ready, with the compliance disclaimer footer built in.
- **Intake & onboarding** — designed invite + signup + email verification + an
  onboarding wizard in plain language (built for low-tech and older clients), including
  insurance-interest capture that lands as a lead in your inbox.
- **Stripe payment links, per advisor** — your services, your prices, your links;
  promo/discount codes supported; clients pay you directly.
- **Share portals** — send a client (or a client's family member) a secure read-only
  link; sensitive fields are server-side sanitized.
- **Referral network page, per advisor** — your vetted contacts (realtors, lenders,
  services) with per-contact disclosure built into the page, so the compliance is done
  right by default.
- **Per-advisor branding** — your name, your contact links, your referral page, your
  pricing, editable in your own Settings.

You don't build any of this, host any of it, or maintain any of it.

## 3. The commission + coaching synergy (why this beats agency-only)

Insurance-only income is feast-or-famine: you hunt, you close, the relationship goes
cold until renewal. The Golden Anchor model keeps the relationship warm and pays three
ways:

1. **Insurance commissions** — your license, your appointments, your commissions. The
   free financial consultation is the most natural insurance door-opener there is: you
   see the family's whole picture, so the coverage conversation is honest and obvious,
   not a cold pitch.
2. **Coaching revenue** — recurring monthly plans and à-la-carte sessions (Golden
   Anchor's own catalog: checkups $149, strategy sessions $129, monthly plans
   $49–79/mo) collected through your own Stripe links. Recurring revenue between
   renewals.
3. **Retention** — a client who logs into *your* portal every month doesn't shop their
   policy elsewhere at renewal. The app is your moat around the book of business.

## 4. Division of labor — crystal clear

| Golden Anchor handles | You bring |
|---|---|
| The software: portal, CRM, reports, intake, calculators, resources — EN/ES | Your insurance license(s) and carrier appointments — you own your compliance |
| Hosting, security, data isolation per advisor (your clients are yours alone) | Your client relationships and your community's trust |
| Stripe payment infrastructure and per-advisor links | Your service pricing and your local market knowledge |
| Bilingual content: resources, useful-links directory, calculators | Your language, your voice with clients |
| Onboarding SOPs, intake questionnaire, coaching playbook | The hustle: consultations, charlas, community presence |
| Product roadmap (client linking, simple mode, WhatsApp channel — in development) | Feedback that shapes the roadmap |

**Compliance boundaries you sign up for:** coaching content stays educational — no
specific securities recommendations, no money management, no credit-repair promises.
Referral relationships are disclosed per contact. We built these rails into the product
so the right way is the easy way.

## 5. Day-1 onboarding outline (pilot agents)

1. **Fit conversation (30 min)** — your book, your languages, your licensing, your
   goals. We're recruiting a small pilot cohort, not mass-hiring. [OWNER: define
   pilot-cohort size — current plan: 2–3 agents.]
2. **Account + tenant setup (same day)** — advisor account created; your branding,
   contact links, and referral page in Settings; your Stripe links connected.
3. **Service menu** — pick your catalog (start from the Golden Anchor default:
   checkup / strategy / quarterly / monthly plans / free insurance consult) and your
   prices; promo codes for your existing clients.
4. **Compliance walkthrough (45 min)** — the education-vs-advice line, referral
   disclosure rules, what the platform does and doesn't let you do. Sign the advisor
   agreement [OWNER: agreement document to be drafted with counsel].
5. **Practice run** — create a test client, run the intake wizard, generate a report,
   send a share portal, take a test payment.
6. **First real client by day 7** — migrate 3–5 existing clients in week one; we
   shadow your first onboarding and fix friction live.
7. **Ongoing** — monthly advisor check-in, feature-request channel, shared playbook of
   what's converting (charlas scripts, consultation flow, leave-behinds).

## 6. Who we're looking for

- Licensed life & health agents (any state; FL network strongest today) or established
  money coaches — bilingual EN/ES strongly preferred.
- Already serving (or wanting to serve) working families, immigrants, older clients —
  the people the big platforms ignore.
- Allergic to the hard-sell. The model is trust-first: education opens the door,
  honesty keeps it open.
